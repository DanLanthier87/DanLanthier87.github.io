The Wendy's Promise

Fresh Beef,
Every Single Day.

Since Dave Thomas opened the first Wendy's in 1969, we've never used frozen beef patties. Every hamburger is made from fresh, never-frozen beef — delivered to each restaurant and formed into patties the same day they're served.

That's not marketing. That's the way it's always been.

The Frosty.
An Icon.

Dave Thomas put the Frosty on the very first Wendy's menu in 1969 — and it hasn't left. A chocolate Frosty is thick enough to eat with a spoon, cool enough to sip, and perfectly calibrated for dipping fries. Chocolate or vanilla, it's the original.
